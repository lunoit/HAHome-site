Apple Intelligence

Your home, explained by AI.

HAHome uses Apple Intelligence to bring on-device AI to your smart home — private, fast, and available on supported iPhone and Mac models.

Free

Energy Insights

Get an AI-generated daily energy analysis — a headline, key observations, an actionable tip to reduce consumption, and an efficiency rating. All processed on-device, no cloud needed.

Pro

Ask About Your Home

Have a natural conversation with your home. Ask questions about device status, get room summaries, check sensor readings, or control lights, climate, covers, media and more — all through plain language.

Pro

Automation Explainer

Don't speak YAML? No problem. Apple Intelligence reads your Home Assistant automations and explains them in plain language — what triggers them, what conditions apply, what actions they perform, and even warns about edge cases. You can also request suggestions for improvement or analyse automations for errors, duplicate conditions, and more.

Apple Intelligence requires a supported iPhone or Mac model with Apple Intelligence enabled. All processing happens on-device — your data never leaves your device.

Accessibility

Designed for everyone.

HAHome is built with accessibility at its core, so every user can control their smart home comfortably.

VoiceOver

Every control, toggle and status is fully labelled for VoiceOver. Navigate your entire smart home by touch or keyboard with descriptive labels that include device names, states and values.

Dynamic Type

All text scales with your preferred text size. Increase the font in iOS or macOS Settings and HAHome adapts throughout — menus, controls, labels and detail views all stay readable.

Voice Control

Use Voice Control to operate HAHome hands-free. All interactive elements are accessible via voice commands, so you can toggle lights, adjust thermostats and trigger scenes without touching the screen.

Dark Interface & High Contrast

The dark interface is designed with sufficient contrast ratios throughout. Status indicators use both colour and icons, so no information is conveyed by colour alone. Light and dark modes are fully supported.
